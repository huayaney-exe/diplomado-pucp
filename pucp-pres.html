The Fundamental Shift

First Principles: What Is a Product?

A product is a transformation engine

It moves people from an undesired state → to a preferred one

People pay for the delta between these states

Problems Have Evolved

In our age of abundance, problems have shifted:

  • Functional: Friction, inefficiency
  • Emotional: Stress, craving, boredom
  • Social: Status, belonging
  • Cognitive: Overload, indecision
  • Existential: Meaning, autonomy
  • Temporal: FOMO, urgency

The Product Power Formula

People don't buy products. They buy better versions of themselves.

Willingness to Pay = ΔState × Emotional Intensity × Problem Frequency

The greater the transformation, the deeper the value.
